IntegrApp

Bringing people together in challenging times

Difficult situations call for generous actions. Luckily, there is always good people around. IntegrApp brings together local residents with refugees to help them integrate into society.

What is IntegrApp about?

The refugee crisis is a very famous topic these days. But it is not a new issue. According to the UN refugee agency, the number of refugees has increased from 38 million to 89 million from 2011 to 2022. There may not be much we can do to reduce these numbers, but we can certainly help the people who are part of this statistic. IntegrApp seeks not only to provide aid to these people, but also to optimize such aid, taking into account the needs of the people by means of AI algorithms.

The idea is that local residents can offer different types of assistance to refugees, and the application keeps track of the most demanded goods. In this way, local residents can find out where the greatest need is, and focus their efforts on meeting those problems. Also, refugees get personalized recommendations depending on the data they input into the app and their activity using it. Help can come in the form of items, social events, courses, or questions and answers. It is intended that this application will not only serve to inform refugees of the local way of life, but also to integrate them into their new society.
