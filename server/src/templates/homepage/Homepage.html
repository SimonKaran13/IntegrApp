What is IntegrApp about?

In 2022, Germany has received 1 million refugees due to the ongoing war in Ukraine. This is not the first time that Germany received a high number of refugees. And yet, until now, refugees still face difficulties in getting their needs, finding information, and integrating into the German culture.
On the other hand, a lot of people want to help those who arrived in Germany seeking refugees. But they also lack the information and resources on how to help.
What if, there is a platform that enables refugees to find help and for local citizens to help them? That is where IntegrApp comes to play: Bringing people together in difficult times
